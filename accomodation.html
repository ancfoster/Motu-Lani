The Residences

From €2000 per night Sleeps up to six

The Residences at Motu Lani represent the pinnacle of Polynesian luxury. Relax in total privacy in your luxurious three bedroom residence, featuring 5000 square feet (464 m2) of living space. 60% of which is air-conditioned interior and 40% outside. Every Resdiecne feature its own private infinity pool.

The Residences feature a large communal living room with an OLED TV, Dolby surround sound system, BlueTooth connectivity and electronic window blinds. There is also a spaciosu dingin arae that can host up to eight. All of the diing options from any of Motu Lani's restaurants can be delivered by your private butler. The residence also features a pantry that is stocked with your favorite beverages and snacks.

The Residences come with three bedrooms. The master bedroom features an Emperor size bed with electric adjustment on each side. The two other bedrooms feature queen beds that can be seperated into two singles, making them ideal for large families. Each bedroom features a TV with satellite connection and Netflix. Each bedroom has stunning views of the lagoon and verandah access.

Each Residence features three en-suite bathrooms with spectatcular lagoon views. The main master ensuite features a beatiful tear-shaped stone bath, his and her basins as well as a seperate shower with waterfall shower head and massage jets.
