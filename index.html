Explore the Island

Motu Lani is a unique private island resort that is part of French Polynesia’s breathtakingly beautiful Tuamotu Archipelago. Motu Lani offers carefree luxury in the midst of pristine nature. With access to the island by private plane, the resort features 24 villas on white-sand beaches frequented by sea turtles, manta rays and exotic birds. The resort was designed to reflect Polynesian lifestyles and culture.
