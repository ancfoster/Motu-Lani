Cuisine

Motu Lani boasts three restaurants, showcasing Polynesian, European and Asian fusion cuisine. All of our menus are designed by our executive chef who has worked in kitchens across Europe and Japan.
You will enjoy the freshest of ingredients as our Chef will select fruits and vegetables naturally grown in the island’s garden. Most fish comes from local waters.

Dining Venues

Elements Restaurant

Elements is the main restaurant on the island, serving authentic Polynesian cuisine for lunch and dinner.
A breakfast buffet with a la carte options is also served here.

East Meets West

A scenic, 15-minute will bring you to Anapa Pearl Farm. Visit a small pearl station where your guide will unveil all the secrets of Tahiti’s mysterious black pearl. Witness the pearl harvest, and see how a black pearl is cultured.

Lumiere

Our fine dining French restaurant offering a changing ten course tasting menu.

Room Service

Twenty-four hour room service is available. Offering full breakfast service, lunch, dinner and hot/cold snacks. Enjoy food from any of the restaurants in your private bungalow or residence.
